History Of Our Company

Greetings From KPR.!

We take immense pleasure in introducing ourselves as one of the leading organizations in India engaged in the manufacturing and marketing of quality Food-based products under the esteemed brand name "KPR". "KPR" has emerged as a trusted name among millions of customers in India, renowned for its top-notch product quality, timely delivery, and competitive pricing. Our journey began with a single production unit with a capacity of producing 300 tons. Presently, we boast three state-of-the-art manufacturing units with a combined capacity of 720 tons. Our expansive product line, which now focuses on Rice, Wheat, and Rice Flours, serves a diverse range of markets. These markets encompass Agriculture, Nutrition, Culinary, and various other applications including Bakery, Confectionery, and Food Processing. Our commitment to quality is evident in our meticulous Quality Control practices. Our Quality Control Laboratory has received training and recognition from esteemed institutions such as the Central Institute of Plastic Engineering and Technology in Chennai, and the Bureau of Indian Standards in Delhi and Coimbatore.
